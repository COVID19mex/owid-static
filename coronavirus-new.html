Current doubling time of cases

As long as cases are doubling the growth is exponential. We explain what this means here.

Based on the global WHO data up to and including 6th March 2020, the doubling time for COVID-19 is as follows:

Doubling time for the global number of cases (excluding China): 4 days

Doubling time for the global number of cases (including China): 21 days

The fact that the doubling time is longer when China is included is due to the fact that the number of daily cases has declined after the lockdown in China.

Why are we showing China separately from the rest of the world?

China is having shutdown measures in place. The rest of the world not. An average between very different situations would not be helpful. It would inform us neither about the situation in China nor about the rest of the world.
